### About me
I am a Ph.D. student in the Joint Doctoral Programme in Interactive and Cognitive Environments (JD ICE) organized by the Centre for Intelligent Sensing at Queen Mary University of London and the Department of Electrical, Electronic, Telecommunications Engineering and Naval Architecture at University of Genova. 

My current research interest involves Artificial Intelligence, Embedded Machine and Deep Learning, Edge Computing, Binary Neural Network, and model optimization for efficient deployment in constrained devices.
